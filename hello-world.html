Hello, World!

Published 21 September, 2017 by Sidney Pham

I've started a blog. I plan on writing (after the HSC, unfortunately) about things like:

  • my disgust at bad UIs (and UX, too) all over the Internet
  • the demerits of mobile-first minimalism
  • Material Design's tackiness
  • gratuitous stock imagery
  • why I don't like the phrases 'with regards to', 'the nature of' and 'utilise'
  • the terrible HSC Software Design & Development course.

And of course, Samuel Davis (of HSC Software Design & Development textbook authorship) will be the target of a lengthy post explaining things like how 'Big Zero Notation' isn't a thing and why maybe HSC students shouldn't be learning about UI design from someone who owns this abomination of a website, where you too can laugh at extortionate $335 ATAR Estimators made in Visual Basic.

You really should expect my arguments to be tenuous and probably biased, so don't say I didn't warn you.
